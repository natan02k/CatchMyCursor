# Cursor Tag 🖱️

Welcome to the simplest multiplayer game on the web. No accounts, no loading screens—just you and everyone else who's online right now.

## How to play
Your mouse cursor is your character. Move it to move, click to interact. It's that simple. 

## The Game Modes
*   **Classic Tag**: One person is "It" (Red). If you're Red, your goal is to click on other cursors to pass the tag. 
    *   *Pro Tip*: As the tagger, click and drag to draw a red line and trap players!
*   **Team Battle**: It's Red vs. Blue (10 vs 10). Click on the other team to eliminate them. The last team standing wins.
*   **Battle Royale**: 50 players, one shrinking circle. Stay inside the zone and knock others out. Last one alive wins.
*   **Zen Mode**: No winning, just vibes. Draw together with others on a giant white canvas.
    *   *Pro Tip*: Right-click to open your **Skill Wheel**. You can freeze others, slow them down, or even flip their controls!

## Controls
- **Move**: Move your mouse.
- **Click**: Tag, eliminate, or use skills.
- **Right Click**: Open the Skill Wheel (Zen Mode only).
- **Navigation**: Switch modes using the bar at the top of the screen.

Have fun!
